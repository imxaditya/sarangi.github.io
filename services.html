Practice Areas

At Sarangi Legal, Advocates and Consultants, we handle a range of legal matters with attention to detail, responsibility, and a deep respect for the law. Our areas of practice include:

Criminal Law

Representation in trials, bail proceedings, appeals, and legal advice in matters under the criminal statutes.

Property Law

Handling of ownership, possession, title, and tenancy-related disputes, including partition suits and injunctions.

Personal Injury

Legal support in claims related to bodily harm, medical negligence, or accidents, focusing on lawful compensation .

Intellectual Property Disputes

We handle full range of Intellectual Property Disputes, from IP prosecution to IP litigation before relevant authorities and courts.

Regulatory Practice

We advise and represent clients before regulators like SEBI, RBI, TRAI, ensuring compliance and resolving sector-specific legal issues efficiently.

Pro Bono Litigation

We take up select matters of public interest or genuine individual need, offering legal support guided by merit, social relevance, and access to justice.

Commercial Law

Assistance in contractual, business, and trade-related legal matters, including dispute resolution and regulatory compliance.

Money Laundering

Legal representation and advisory under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act (PMLA), including enforcement actions and proceedings.

Drug Offence

Defending clients in Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act matters, including bail, seizure disputes, and trial-stage representation.

Corporate Litigation

Disputes involving shareholders, directors, company operations, and regulatory non-compliance before NCLT and other forums.

Arbitration

Domestic and international arbitration support, from drafting of agreements to representation before the arbitral tribunals.

Insolvency & Bankruptcy

Advising creditors and corporate debtors under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), including appearances before the NCLT.

Real Estate Disputes

Assistance in builder-buyer disputes, Real Estate Act, 2016 proceedings, delayed possession claims, and title issues.

Writ Litigation

Filing and defending writ petitions under Articles 32 and 226 concerning fundamental rights, service law, and administrative action.

Consumer Disputes

Representation in matters involving defective goods, deficient services, and unfair trade practices before consumer forums.
