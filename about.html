ABOUT

The Firm

Founded on Friendship, Built on Trust, Driven by Law

Established in 2017, our firm is the result of a shared vision by close friends and classmates from the prestigious Campus Law Centre (CLC), University of Delhi. With a common goal to redefine client-focused legal services, we came together to build Sarangi Legal, that upholds integrity, dedication, and excellence at every level.

Today, we are proud to be recognized as a full-service law firm delivering comprehensive legal solutions across a broad spectrum of practice areas. From corporate advisory and litigation to intellectual property, real estate, family law, criminal defense, and dispute resolution — we handle it all with precision and care.

Our Journey and Approach

What began as a modest setup fueled by ambition and camaraderie has grown into a thriving legal practice trusted by individuals, startups, SMEs, and large enterprises alike. With every case we take, we stay rooted in the values that brought us together: commitment to justice, respect for clients, and an unwavering pursuit of excellence. We believe in offering client-centric, result-oriented legal counsel. Whether you’re navigating a complex business deal or facing a personal legal challenge, our team brings clarity, strategy, and relentless advocacy to the table.
