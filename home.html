Practice Areas

At Sarangi Legal, we handle a range of legal matters with attention to detail, responsibility, and a deep respect for the law. Our areas of practice include:

Criminal Law

Representation in trials, bail proceedings, appeals, and legal advice in matters under the criminal statutes.

Intellectual Property Disputes

We handle full range of Intellectual Property Disputes, from IP prosecution to IP litigation.

Personal Injury

Legal support in claims related to bodily harm, medical negligence, or accidents, focusing on lawful compensation .

Commercial Law

Assistance in contractual, business, and trade-related legal matters, including dispute resolution and regulatory compliance.

Money Laundering

Legal representation and advisory under the Prevention of Money Laundering Act, including enforcement actions and proceedings.

Drug Offence

Defending clients in Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act matters, including bail, seizure disputes, and trial-stage representation.
